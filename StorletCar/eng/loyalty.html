LOYALTY PROGRAM

Free car rental. Do you think it does not exist?

You are wrong!

We have developed a unique loyalty system specifically for Storlet Car Rental clients. When you rent a car and receive a Storlet Car Rental Privilege card, you start earning miles in the amount of at least 10% of the daily rental cost. The longer the rental period, the more miles on your card. 1 mile = 1 ruble. You can redeem miles at any of our rental points in any city and any country in the world where our company has representative offices. Just come and rent a car for free!

HOW IT WORKS?

  • When you contact our company for the first time, you receive a card that has already been credited with welcome 500 miles (which is equal to 500 rubles). Welcome miles are credited regardless of which car you take and for how long. If you apply again, while ordering please indicate the card number. The card is individual and assigned to only one person or company.

  • During the car rental period, at least 10% of the daily car rental cost will be charged to the first welcome 500 miles every day. For example, if you rent a car for 2000 rubles per day and use it 10 days, you will receive at least 200 bonus miles on your card every day. This means that in 10 days your card will have 2500 miles (2500 rubles) which you can spend on car rental.

  • The next time you contact any of our rental stations (in any city of any country in the world), you will be able to spend the accumulated miles and rent a car for free! Based on our example, you can rent a car for free for a day and still get a discount of 500 rubles the next day, that is, rent a car for two days for only 1500 rubles. Substantial savings!

  • Please note that it is possible to pay with miles only for rental services, that is, they cannot be used to pay for additional equipment or a deposit. In addition, you can rent a car for miles only if the rental period is at least a day. If you use services of our company in different countries, and miles are accumulated on the card in different currencies, then in the future they can be converted into a single currency at the rate of the Central Bank of the Russian Federation on the day of payment.

  • Each client can get acquainted with the history of crediting and spending the accumulated miles in their personal account. The time for accumulating, storing and using miles is unlimited. That is, you can accumulate miles for a whole year, and spend them, for example, on vacation abroad. Every time, after the end of the car rental period, a report on the number of credited miles is sent to the client's e-mail.

  • The way Storlet Car Rental Privilege works resembles the cash-back system when you make a purchase and 10–20% of the cost is returned back to the card. You can use the accumulated miles in any city in the world where our rental stations operate. Storlet Car Rental is the only company that offers such a loyalty system. Only here you can get a car for free.
